My Poem About Napoleon

Napoleon Bonaparte was a great little man

A battle he lost was Trafalgar

Poor Napoleon how much he did lose

Oh poor Napoleon at Waterloo too

Later about that but before then

Everyone with him the government they overthrew

Oh how when you reached Moscow everything was burned

Now you must retreat, for food they yearned


Borodino thou didst barely complete

Oh your success with the plebicite

Now you can reign emperor from your height

An advance from brittain made you close many ports

Perhaps this will settle the feud.

Ah but it did not, as the Continental system did retort

Right, as thou has been exiled once to Elba

Thou wast exiled again to St. Helena.

Everday, thou wilst be remembered!
