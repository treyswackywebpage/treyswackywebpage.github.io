Seahorses



Dwarf

Spiny
  • Third smallest seahorses species
    in the world
  • Found in seagrass beds in the Gulf
    of Mexico and many other places
  • Their lifespan is 1 year and grow up to
    1 inch
  • Can reach a maximum length of
    15-17 cm
  • The body is slender, elongated
    and completely covered with thorns
  • The snout is very long and tapered
    and is generally striped with one or
    more thin white lines
