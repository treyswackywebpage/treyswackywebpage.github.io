More about Roblox

Roblox has a vast array of minigames and different worlds you can join. There are so many and the list goes on and on. On these worlds you can meet other playes and communicate with them and make friends.

This is some gameplay from the game

One of the most played type of world is called an obby. Obby is short for obstacle course. The obstacle courses found in roblox usually consist of you jumping over or avoiding red blocks. If you touch a red block you will have to restart the obstacle course.
