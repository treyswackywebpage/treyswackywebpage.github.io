Different bosses

These bosses are in a list from hardest to easiest although I will only be putting 5 bosses on this list.

  1. The Moon Lord

    2. The Moon Lord is the most difficult boss in Terraria. He is basically a floating body with arms and a head.

  2. Duke Fishron

    3. Duke Fishron is a hard boss. He can be summoned using a shrimpy truffle as bait on a fishing rod. He is a big fish that flies around and hits you.

  3. Plantera

    4. Plantera is a big plant the can be summoned while in the jungle biome. It lauches spike balls and can mover through blocks.

  4. Golem

    5. Golem is a big stone block that wants to kill you. It shoots lasers and tries to stomp on you. It can be summoned at a lizhard altar using a powercell.

  5. Queen Bee

    6. Queen Bee is a big bee that fires stingers at you. Its pretty scary and decently difficult. I can be summoned by breaking a queen larva in a hive found in the jungle.
