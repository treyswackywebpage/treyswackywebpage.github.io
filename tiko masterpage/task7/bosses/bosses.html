Different bosses

This is a list of bosses in Terraria. However, I will only be putting 5 bosses on this list.

  • The Moon Lord

    • The Moon Lord is the most difficult boss in Terraria. He is basically a floating body with arms and a head.

  • Duke Fishron

    • Duke Fishron is a hard boss. He can be summoned using a shrimpy truffle as bait on a fishing rod. He is a big fish that flies around and hits you.

  • Plantera

    • Plantera is a big plant the can be summoned while in the jungle biome. It lauches spike balls and can mover through blocks.

  • Golem

    • Golem is a big stone block that wants to kill you. It shoots lasers and tries to stomp on you. It can be summoned at a lizhard altar using a powercell.

  • Queen Bee

    • Queen Bee is a big bee that fires stingers at you. Its pretty scary and decently difficult. I can be summoned by breaking a queen larva in a hive found in the jungle.
