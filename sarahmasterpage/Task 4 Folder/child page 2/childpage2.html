The human eyes sees using cones and rods. The cones in your eyes are the reason that we can see in color. The more or less cones an organism has is responsible for the amount of color that they can see. Rods are the reason that we can see in the dark. They are the reason that we can have night vision or how cirtain animals see better in the dark than others.

The cones work by sensing light waves. When they detect a cirtain wavelength it will send a signal to the brain and the brain interprets the signals.
