Eye Colors Around the World

Eye color is one of the main ways that people will identity each other.
While we are told that there is a wide range of eye colors, they are shown
in only a few rare cases. Every color exept for brown that we see in eyes has
come from a genetic mutation many years ago. Brown eyes are the most common
and are the only color that has not mutated. Brown eyes make up more than 50% of
all eye colors.


Eye Colors in Books

While in reality the range of colors is less than expected. It is even less varied
in books and movies. For the world brown eyes are over 50% of the population, but in
books blue eyes are the most commonly mentioned at 52% dispite only making up 8% of the world population.
