White Teacup Pomeranian

A white teacup pomeranian is a teacup poodle mixed with a pomeranian. Becasue of its small size and teacup genetics it will remain looking like a puppy for its entire life. The teacup pomeranian can live up to 12 years old compared to larger dogs. The dog is very friendly and perky. It is good with kids and can be used as an alarm dog.





Australian Shepherd Puppy

An An Australian Shepherd is a purebred dog which used to be used to herd animals. Becasue of it's herding instinct it can tend to nip at the heals of your family if they stray away from the main group. They can be trained to stop biting and are very friendly to children.




German Shepherd Puppy

German Shepherds are amazing fluffballs. They are friendly when you get to know them but they can be aggressive if they think you will try to hurt them. They are purebred and are amazing companions for anyone who needs a loyal buddy.
