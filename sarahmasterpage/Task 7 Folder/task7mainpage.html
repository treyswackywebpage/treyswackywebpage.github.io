Colors and the Effect they have on your Mood

Link to Mainpage

Colors cause people to feel certain things.
They can make us feel sad or happy or angry for no other reason than the color.

Our brain enterprets the different wavelengths of light
as colors. The rays of light go through our cornea and into
the color cones inside our eyes. The cones send signals
to our brain that it interprets as colors.
Becasue our brain uses chemicals to 'see' the world
around us then color can make us feel in different
ways.

The reason that our brains interpret colors with a mood and feeling
is because of hormones and chemicals. When you brain recieves the
sensors from the color cones it releases chemicals to create
the image that you see. The chemicals that it releases are
directally connected to you mood. Humans interpret yellow
as a happy color because when it recieves the sensor for yellow
your brain releases seratonin- one of the key chemicals in happiness.
